Data files description

cemeteries.csv - list of all cemeteries in poland according to dbpedia
people_data.csv - list of all people from dbpedia with basic info (date of death,
cemetery of burial etc). Note, dbpedia seems to be incomplete, new data must be extracted directly from wiki pages.
people_data-*.csv - extra files limited to geography for faster processing
occupation - occupation of people from people_data.csv, according do dbpedia
person_score.csv - scores of the people popularity. Final score value is last colum (score)
graves.csv - list of located graves from some warsaw cemeteries, with lat lon (last columns)
